Herschel Gould Belt Survey

Maps of filaments in nearby clouds (350 micro-m) from Koch & Rosolowsky (submitted). All data shown here is publicly released by the HGBS consortium

The skeletons indicate the positions of the detected filaments in each region. Red indicates the filaments found using the original image, while blue are the filaments found by convolving and regridding each map to a common resolution and pixel scale.
